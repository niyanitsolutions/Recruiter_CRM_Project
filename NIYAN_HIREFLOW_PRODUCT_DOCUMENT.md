# SECTION 2 — ABOUT THE PRODUCT

**Niyan HireFlow CRM** is a modern, cloud-based Recruitment and Human Resource Management platform purpose-built for recruitment agencies, staffing companies, HR consultancies, and growing businesses that need a centralized, intelligent system to manage their entire talent pipeline — from sourcing candidates all the way through to employee onboarding, payroll, and performance.

Unlike generic CRMs that force you to adapt your hiring process to fit the software, Niyan HireFlow is built around the actual workflows of recruitment professionals — making it intuitive, fast, and genuinely powerful from day one.

At its heart, Niyan HireFlow CRM answers a single critical business question:

> "How do we place the right talent, with the right client, at the right time — and track every step of the journey without losing data or momentum?"

The platform creates a single, unified workspace where recruiters, HR managers, account managers, and business owners all operate from the same data — eliminating silos, reducing manual work, and accelerating hiring outcomes.

# SECTION 3 — PROBLEMS BUSINESSES FACE

## The Real Cost of Unorganized Recruitment

Most recruitment teams operate in a chaotic mix of spreadsheets, email threads, WhatsApp messages, and disconnected tools. This approach creates serious business problems — and most businesses don't even realize how much money and opportunity they're losing.

### Problem 1 — Data Scattered Everywhere

**The Pain:** Candidate information lives in Excel files. Client contacts sit in email. Interview feedback arrives over WhatsApp. Offer details are on a shared Google Sheet. When someone asks "What's the status of this candidate?", nobody has the full picture.

**The Cost:** Hours wasted daily in status meetings. Deals lost because follow-ups were missed. Candidates dropped because nobody remembered to follow up.

**Niyan HireFlow Solves This:** Every candidate, job, client, application, interview, and offer lives in one centralized platform. Any authorized team member can see the complete picture in seconds — with full history.

### Problem 2 — Manual Follow-Up = Missed Opportunities

**The Pain:** Interview reminders are forgotten. Offer validity deadlines pass unnoticed. A candidate who joined 90 days ago should trigger a payout — but nobody remembered.

**The Cost:** Lost revenue from missed payout windows. Strained client relationships from lack of follow-through.

**Niyan HireFlow Solves This:** Automated reminders trigger at Day 10, Day 30, and Day 90 after a candidate joins. Offer expiry alerts fire automatically. Interview reminders go out without anyone lifting a finger.

### Problem 3 — No Visibility Into Team Performance

**The Pain:** Managers have no real-time insight into which coordinators are active, which jobs are aging, which clients are underserved, or which sourcing channels are actually delivering.

**The Cost:** Underperforming team members go unchecked. Best-performing channels get no extra investment. Bad hiring patterns repeat because there's no data to learn from.

**Niyan HireFlow Solves This:** A live analytics dashboard tracks placements, application funnel conversion, coordinator activity, client revenue, and source effectiveness — all in real time, with exportable reports.

### Problem 4 — Hiring Delays From Poor Coordination

**The Pain:** Scheduling interviews requires back-and-forth between coordinators, clients, and candidates. Round 1 results don't automatically trigger Round 2 scheduling. Documents for onboarding are chased manually.

**The Cost:** Candidates drop out during slow hiring processes. Clients lose confidence. Time-to-hire stretches from weeks to months.

**Niyan HireFlow Solves This:** Multi-round interview pipelines are pre-configured. Passing a round automatically opens the next. Document checklists are auto-assigned during onboarding. The entire workflow runs on structured stages — not memory.

### Problem 5 — Partner & Commission Chaos

**The Pain:** Recruitment agencies that work with external partner consultants struggle to track who referred which candidate, what the commission percentage is, and when the 45/60/90-day payout window opens.

**The Cost:** Commission disputes. Delayed payments. Partners losing trust and taking candidates elsewhere.

**Niyan HireFlow Solves This:** Every application carries its source and partner reference. Payout eligibility is automatically calculated from the candidate's joining date. Invoices are generated in the system, and payment status is tracked end-to-end.

### Problem 6 — Security & Access Control Gaps

**The Pain:** In most agencies, everyone sees everything. Partners can see client details they shouldn't. Junior coordinators can edit data they shouldn't touch. There's no audit trail when data goes missing or changes unexpectedly.

**The Cost:** Data breaches. Confidential client information leaks. Compliance failures. No accountability when errors happen.

**Niyan HireFlow Solves This:** 60+ granular permissions, 8 system roles, and unlimited custom roles ensure every user sees exactly what they need — and nothing more. A complete audit trail logs every login, every data change, and every sensitive action.

### Problem 7 — HR and Recruitment Completely Disconnected

**The Pain:** When a candidate joins as an employee, the information has to be manually re-entered into the HR system. Leave requests are managed separately. Payroll is calculated elsewhere.

**The Cost:** Duplicate data entry. Errors from re-keying. Time wasted on administrative work that adds no value.

**Niyan HireFlow Solves This:** A single click converts a joined candidate into an employee record. Attendance, leave, payroll, and performance management are all part of the same platform — fully connected to the original recruitment journey.

## MODULE 4 — APPLICATION TRACKING

> "Know exactly where every candidate stands — at every moment."

### What It Does
The Application module is the bridge between a candidate and a job. Every time a candidate is considered for a position, an application is created — and that application flows through a structured status pipeline with complete history tracking.

### Key Capabilities

**14-Stage Application Pipeline:** Applications move through a clear, structured journey:

```
Applied → Eligible → Screening → Shortlisted → Interview →
Next Round → Selected → Offered → Offer Accepted → Offer Declined →
Joined → Rejected → Withdrawn → On Hold
```

Every stage transition is recorded with the user who made the change, the timestamp, and any remarks — creating an immutable audit trail.

**Auto-Eligibility Scoring:** When a candidate is applied to a job with eligibility criteria, the system automatically calculates a match score (0–100%) based on skills, experience, location, CTC, and education.

**Rejection with Reason Tracking:** Rejections capture why — not qualified, overqualified, salary mismatch, notice period issue, failed interview, background check, offer declined, no-show, or position closed — enabling pattern analysis and process improvement.

**Offer Management:** When a candidate is selected, the offer details (CTC, designation, location, offer date, validity) are recorded directly in the application — creating a single source of truth.

**Joining & DOJ Tracking:** Expected and actual joining dates are tracked. When a candidate joins, the system automatically links the application to the onboarding module for continued tracking.

### Business Benefits
- Give every team member complete visibility into application status
- Eliminate "I thought someone else was following up" situations
- Build institutional knowledge from rejection patterns
- Generate accurate placement success metrics

> "The placement doesn't end at the offer. It ends at the payout."

The Onboarding module manages everything that happens after a candidate accepts an offer — through their joining, their first 90 days, and the payout to your team or partner. It is the financial completion of every placement.

### Key Capabilities

**Offer-to-Joining Workflow:** The moment an offer is accepted, an onboarding record is created with offer CTC, designation, and location, offer release date and validity window, expected Date of Joining (DOJ), payout timeline (45, 60, or 90 days after joining), and offer letter document attachment.

**Automated Reminder System:** The system automatically sends reminders at key milestones: Before DOJ (joining confirmation reminder), Day 10 (early check-in), Day 30 (mid-point review), and Day 45/60/90 (payout eligibility notification to accounts team).

**Payout Eligibility Automation:** The system calculates payout eligibility automatically based on the candidate's actual joining date and the configured payout timeline. The accounts team receives an alert when a placement is ready for billing.

**Status Lifecycle:**
```
Offer Released → Offer Accepted → DOJ Confirmed →
DOJ Extended (if applicable) → Joined → Payout Eligible → Completed
```

**Invoice Generation:** Raise client invoices directly from the onboarding record — with placement details, commission amounts, and payment terms pre-filled.

### Business Benefits

## MODULE 14 — SECURITY & COMPLIANCE

> "Enterprise-grade security built into every layer."

### Security Features

**Authentication & Session Management:** Secure login with email and password. Account lockout after multiple failed login attempts. Concurrent session prevention — only one active session per user. Session expiry and automatic logout. IP address logging for every login event.

**Password Policies:** Minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numeric requirements. Forced password change for new users (configurable). Password history prevention. Admin-triggered password resets.

**Audit Trail:** Every single action in the platform is logged — who performed the action, what they did (created, updated, deleted, viewed), which record was affected, when the action occurred, and from which IP address. Audit logs are immutable — they cannot be modified or deleted.

**Data Isolation (Multi-Tenancy):** Each company's data is stored in a completely separate, isolated database. No data from one company is ever visible or accessible to another company — at any level.

**Soft Delete Architecture:** Nothing is truly deleted. Records that are "deleted" by users are moved to an archived state — recoverable by administrators and visible in deletion history. This prevents accidental permanent data loss.

### Security Features Table

| Protection | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Session Security** | Each user can only have one active session at a time |
| **Login Protection** | Account automatically locked after multiple failed attempts |
| **IP Logging** | Every login event records the user's IP address |
| **Audit Trail** | Every create, update, and delete action is logged permanently |
| **Data Isolation** | Each company's data lives in a completely separate database |
| **Soft Delete** | Deleted records are archived, not permanently removed |
| **Encrypted Credentials** | SMTP passwords and sensitive configuration values stored encrypted |
| **Password Policies** | Configurable: minimum length, complexity, expiry, history |

# SECTION 5 — DASHBOARD ANALYSIS

## How Work Actually Flows Through the System

### WORKFLOW 1 — The Full Recruitment Lifecycle

```
STEP 1: Client Requirement Received
Coordinator creates a new Job record under the client.
Sets job details, eligibility criteria, priority, and coordinator assignment.

STEP 2: Candidate Sourcing

Partners submit candidates through their portal.
Source attribution recorded automatically.

STEP 3: Application Created
Coordinator applies candidate to the job.
System auto-scores match (0-100%) based on eligibility criteria.
Application status: APPLIED → AUTO-SCORED.

STEP 4: Screening
Coordinator reviews profile and match score.
Moves qualified candidates: SCREENING → SHORTLISTED.
Rejected candidates: status updated with reason recorded.

STEP 5: Interview Pipeline Activated
First interview round scheduled (date, time, mode, interviewer).
Automated notifications sent to candidate and interviewer.
Application moves to: INTERVIEW.

STEP 6: Interview Round Executed
Interview conducted on scheduled date.
Interviewer submits structured feedback with rating and decision.

STEP 7: Round Outcome
PASS: System opens next round scheduling.
FAIL: Candidate notified; application moves to REJECTED with reason.
HOLD: Application parked for later consideration.

STEP 8: Final Round Complete → Selection
All rounds passed: application status → SELECTED.
Coordinator notified: proceed to offer stage.

STEP 9: Offer Released
Offer details logged: CTC, designation, location, offer date, validity.
Offer letter uploaded to system. Candidate notified: OFFER RELEASED.

STEP 10: Offer Outcome
Accepted: Application → OFFER ACCEPTED; Onboarding record auto-created.
Declined: Application → OFFER DECLINED; decline reason recorded.

STEP 11: Onboarding Begins
Expected Date of Joining confirmed. Document checklist assigned.
Automated reminder sent before DOJ.

STEP 12: Candidate Joins
Status → JOINED; actual DOJ recorded.
Day 10 and Day 30 reminder timers started automatically.
If converting to employee: HR creates employee record from onboarding record.

STEP 13: Payout Tracking
System counts days from actual joining date.
At Day 45/60/90: Payout Eligible alert sent to accounts team.
Invoice raised against client. Partner commission calculated and payout initiated.

COMPLETE: Placement revenue realized, data archived, talent pool updated.
```

# SECTION 7 — ROLE-BASED ACCESS & SECURITY

## The Right Access for the Right Person

One of the most critical — and most often overlooked — elements of a business software platform is controlling who sees what. Niyan HireFlow CRM is built with a deeply layered access control system that protects data, creates accountability, and gives every user a clean, focused workspace.

## How Role-Based Access Works

When a user logs into Niyan HireFlow:
- Their role is identified
- Their permissions (from role + any individual overrides) are loaded
- The navigation menu shows only modules they're authorized to access
- API calls for restricted data are blocked at the server level — not just hidden in the UI
- Every action they perform is logged with their identity

## Role Access Summary

**Owner & Admin:** Full platform access — every module, every record, all settings. Can create, edit, and delete any data. Can manage users, roles, and permissions. Can view all audit logs and reports.

**Candidate Coordinator:** Can add, edit, and manage candidate profiles. Can create applications and move them through the pipeline. Can schedule and manage interviews. Cannot access financial modules (payouts, invoices, commissions).

**Client Coordinator:** Can add, edit, and manage client accounts and job mandates. Can view candidate applications on their assigned jobs.

**HR (Onboarding & HRM):** Full access to the onboarding module. Full HRM access: employees, attendance, leave, payroll, performance.

**Accounts Team:** Access to payouts, invoices, commissions, and financial reports. Can view onboarding records for payout eligibility.

**Partner (External):** Limited portal: only sees jobs shared with them. Can only view and manage their own candidate submissions. Can view commission statements and invoice history.

**Employee (HRM Self-Service):** Can view and manage their own profile, attendance, leave, and payslip. Cannot access other employees' records.

**Manager (HRM):** All employee self-service capabilities. Can approve/reject leave requests for their team. Can view team attendance and conduct performance reviews.

## Security Protections

| Protection | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Session Security** | Each user can only have one active session. Logging in from a new device ends all previous sessions. |
| **Login Protection** | After multiple failed login attempts, the account is automatically locked. |
| **IP Logging** | Every login event records the user's IP address for security review. |
| **Audit Trail** | Every create, update, and delete action is logged permanently. |
| **Data Isolation** | Each company's data lives in a completely separate database — no cross-contamination possible. |
| **Soft Delete** | Deleted records are archived, not permanently removed — recoverable by authorized admins. |
| **Encrypted Credentials** | SMTP passwords and sensitive configuration values are stored encrypted. |
| **Password Policies** | Configurable: minimum length, complexity, expiry, history, and forced change on first login. |

When a candidate is applied to a job, the system automatically calculates an eligibility match score (0–100%) based on: skill match (mandatory vs. preferred skills), years of experience (within required range), location preference, education level, CTC expectations vs. offered range, and notice period vs. maximum acceptable.

**Business Impact:** Coordinators no longer read through profiles and make judgment calls alone. They see ranked, scored candidates — and focus their time on the highest-potential matches.

## Automated Email Notifications

The system sends professionally formatted emails automatically when a user account is created (welcome email), an interview is scheduled (candidate and interviewer notifications), an offer is released, a document is verified or rejected, a leave application is approved or rejected, a payout becomes eligible, an invoice is raised or approved, and when a password reset is requested.
